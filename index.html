Overview

Learn how to achieve financial independence by adopting a cash-first lifestyle, avoiding debt, and rejecting traditional banking systems.

Core Principles

  • Avoid borrowing for all expenses, including homes and automobiles.
  • Rent until you can afford to buy in cash.
  • Live within your means at all times.
  • Build a cash-first savings plan.
  • Reject traditional banks and credit scores.
