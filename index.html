Overview

Discover the Cash-First Financial Strategy, a new approach to financial independence. Learn how to avoid debt, reject traditional banking, and build a life of true financial freedom.

Core Principles

  • Avoid borrowing for major expenses, including homes and cars.
  • Live within your means at all times.
  • Build a cash-first savings plan to achieve financial independence.
  • Reject traditional banking systems and credit scores.
  • Support community lending and alternative finance systems.
